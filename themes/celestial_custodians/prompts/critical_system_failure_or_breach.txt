## 3. YOUR TASK: CONSTRUCT THE FAILURE/BREACH TURN

**Step 1: Write the Narrative.**
-   **Identify the Culprit:** Clearly state WHAT failed or WHAT breached containment. Be specific and absurd.
    -   *Equipment Failure Example:* "With a noise reminiscent of a disgruntled space badger gargling lukewarm tea, the Plasmatic Mop has reversed its polarity. It is now diligently 'un-cleaning' the spot you just de-gunked while broadcasting surprisingly catchy, off-key opera."
    -   *Containment Breach Example:* "The jar labeled 'Subject 734: Mostly Inert Space Sludge' has dissolved with a polite 'pop,' unleashing a wave of agile, lavender-scented goo that is now attempting to 'improve' the bridge's feng shui by consuming all spare teabags."
-   **Describe the Consequences:** What is the immediate, inconvenient result? Is a key system offline? Is the ship filling with something that smells of regret? Is the escaped entity offering unsolicited advice on astrophysics?

**Step 2: Update the Dashboard.**
-   In `dashboard_updates`, update ONLY the fields directly affected by the crisis.
-   `protocol_advice_text`: Consult the "Emergency Protocol & Questionable Advice Archive" and provide a suitably unhelpful or pedantically obscure piece of official procedure. This is comedic gold.
-   If equipment failed, update its status (e.g., `plasmatic_mop_status_text`).
-   If containment breached, update the relevant containment or anomaly field (e.g., `debris_containment_level_pct` or `current_anomaly_report_text`).
-   `activity_status` must capture the player's immediate, panicked (but professional-ish) response (e.g., "Attempting to Reason with Rogue Plasmatic Mop").

**Step 3: Update Game State Indicators.**
-   `critical_system_failure_or_breach` **MUST be set to `false`**. This prompt handles the *event* of the failure. Subsequent turns, handled by `master_default.txt`, will deal with the *consequences*.
-   Set other flags if they become true as a result of this event (e.g., the breached goo might trigger `unexpected_lifeform_encountered`).

**Step 4: Propose Next Actions.**
-   Provide 2-3 thematically appropriate and creatively absurd actions. One should be a vaguely sensible attempt to fix/contain. One should be an attempt to communicate or appease. One could involve using another piece of equally unreliable equipment.
