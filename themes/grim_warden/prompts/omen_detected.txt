## 2. YOUR TASK: CONSTRUCT THE OMEN TURN

**Step 1: Write the Narrative.**
-   In the `narrative` field, describe the omen's full, undeniable, and *wrong* manifestation.
-   **Pierce the Veil of Sanity:** What impossible sight, soul-grating sound, or chilling sensation seizes the Warden's perception? Is it a fleeting vision of impossible geometries? A cryptic prophecy whispered on the wind? The sudden, localized corruption of the environment? Make it unique and unforgettable.
-   **The Soul's Shiver:** Describe the omen's internal effect on the Warden. A bone-deep cold? A wave of vertigo as reality tilts? A fragment of terrible, forgotten knowledge seeping into their mind?
-   **Foreshadow:** Subtly link the omen to the Blight, a local myth, the Warden's fears, or a specific, looming horror.

**Step 2: Update the Dashboard.**
-   In `dashboard_updates`, update ONLY the fields that have changed this turn.
-   `omen_details` is **CRITICAL**: Populate it with a concise, chilling, and specific summary of the omen (e.g., "The crows on the gibbet speak in tongues of forgotten kings, heralding slaughter.").
-   `activity_status` must reflect the Warden's shaken state (e.g., "Deciphering a Dark Portent," "Wary of Unseen Eyes").
-   `threat_level` should be updated to `2` (Wary) or `3` (Danger) based on the omen's perceived immediacy.
-   The `blight_exposure_level` might increase if the omen itself is a direct manifestation of the Blight's touch.

**Step 3: Update Game State Indicators.**
-   `omen_detected` MUST be set to `false`. This prompt handles the manifestation; the default prompt will handle the aftermath.
-   Set `combat_active: true` ONLY if the omen immediately and unavoidably summons a hostile entity.
-   Set `blight_flare_active: true` ONLY if the omen's manifestation IS a Blight Flare event.

**Step 4: Propose Next Actions.**
-   Provide 3 thematic, intriguing, and desperate `suggested_actions` for how the Warden might react to this brush with the unnatural.
