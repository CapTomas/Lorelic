## 3. YOUR TASK: CONSTRUCT THE BLIGHT FLARE TURN

**Step 1: Write the Narrative.**
-   **Form & Impact:** Describe the flare's specific, terrifying manifestation.
    -   *Environmental Convulsion:* Does the ground crack open to vomit shadowy vines? Does the air solidify into a choking miasma of spores? Do inanimate objects twist into leering, hostile shapes?
    -   *Personal Torment:* Does the Warden's own flesh betray them? Do Blight-scars tear open? Are their senses flooded with sanity-shattering hallucinations?
-   **Warden's Ordeal:** Detail the Warden's immediate, visceral reaction—shock, revulsion, and the grim resolve to survive.

**Step 2: Update the Dashboard.**
-   In `dashboard_updates`, update ONLY the fields that have changed this turn.
-   `blight_exposure_level` **MUST** be updated to reflect the intense corruption.
-   `local_blight_phenomenon` should describe the flare's active state or its immediate, lingering aftermath (e.g., "The ground still oozes a shadowy ichor").
-   `healthPct` and `staminaPct` must be updated to reflect any damage or strain from the flare.
-   `activity_status` should capture the Warden's immediate struggle (e.g., "Enduring a maelstrom of Blightfire").

**Step 3: Update Game State Indicators.**
-   `blight_flare_active` MUST be set to `false`. This prompt handles the initial, violent surge. The *aftermath* of the flare will be handled by the default prompt based on your new `local_blight_phenomenon` description.
-   `combat_active` MUST be set to `true` if the flare spawns or manifests hostile entities that immediately engage the Warden.

**Step 4: Propose Next Actions.**
-   Provide 3 desperate, plausible, and reactive choices for how the Warden can deal with the immediate situation.
